Make. Change.

We're creating products and services that answer unmet needs for people who are under served by traditional banking. We're a design team that measures our impact in products shipped, goals met, and lives changed.

We Make innovative banking alternative products and services like Serve and Bluebird. We also support the design and development of new products and services in emerging markets.

Here's how we like to work:

Collaboration

The EG team is a small, tight-knit group of people with different areas of focus and different ways of communicating. Designers work closely with product owners and technologists to develop solutions collaboratively.

Communication

EG designers talk with their hands. We whiteboard, sketch, doodle, draw, mockup, diagram, and code to communicate ideas.

User Centered

Our users are real people with real needs. EG design is user-lead. We listen to our users. We watch how they use our products, and we design solutions that help them transact the way they want.

Process

We never make giant wireframe decks or enormous style guides. We have ownership of our work and the flexibility to iterate often. We work as a team to understand user needs and design thoughtful solutions. We use sketches and our robust prototyping environment to communicate the user experience.

Experience Designer

Experience designers are systems thinkers that can contribute to a design process from start to finish. This means leading discovery activities, forming insights, developing both functional and visual design, and communicating that design to the various people who make it real. Our XD's have a range of backgrounds and never work alone. They're process driven, work with their hands, and are excellent ambassadors for design across American Express.

Experience Technologist

Experience Technologists with a front-end development background collaborate with the Experience Designers to co-design our user experiences within the medium in which they will be experienced. By designing in code we ensure that all interactions look and feel great on every device and we arrive to these designs more efficiently.

Content Strategist

Our Content Strategists not only shape the tone of our service, they consider how content takes shape across our experience. They support our products and services by creating content guidelines, writing clear and engaging copy, and collaborating with teams across Enterprise Growth. Our Content Strategists have experience in writing for the web and are strong design thinkers.
