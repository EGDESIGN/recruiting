At Enterprise Growth, we’re changing the face of American Express.

Make. Change.

We're creating products and services that answer unmet needs for people who are under served by traditional banking. We're a design team that measures our impact in products shipped, goals met, and lives changed, not PowerPoint.

Things that we make

We make innovative banking alternative products and services like Serve and Bluebird. Our team also supports the design and development of new products and services in emerging markets. Here's how we like to work:

Collaboration

The EG team is a tight-knit group of people with different areas of focus and different ways of communicating, but we all talk with our hands. We whiteboard, sketch, doodle, draw, mockup, diagram, and code to work collaboratively with product owners, development, and business partners and communicate ideas.

User Centered

Our users are real people with real needs. EG design is user-led. We listen to our users. We watch how they use our products, and we design solutions that help them transact the way they want. The backbone of our approach is a traditional user-centered design model.

Process

We never make giant wireframe decks or enormous style guides. We have ownership of our work and the flexibility to iterate often through Agile. We work as a team to understand user needs and design thoughtful solutions. We use sketches and our robust prototyping environment to communicate the user experience.

Our Team

Whether showing off our culinary skills at our monthly potluck or leading mini improve comedy workshops, we create opportunities to weave our passions into our work.

EG designers are a passionate group of people with a diverse set of backgrounds and skills. We are poets, painters, teachers, actors, and florists. We are also designers, strategists, technologists and managers.

XD

Design Everything

Experience designers are systems thinkers that can contribute to a design process from start to finish. This means leading discovery activities, forming insights, developing both functional and visual design, and communicating that design to the various people who make it real. Our XD’s have a range of backgrounds and never work alone. They’re process driven, work with their hands, and are excellent ambassadors for design across American Express.

XT

Nerd City

Experience Technologists with a front-end development background collaborate with the Experience Designers to co-design our user experiences within the medium in which they will be experienced. By designing in code we ensure that all interactions look and feel great on every device and we arrive to these designs more efficiently.

CS

Wine in the Wine Glass

Our Content Strategists not only shape the tone of our service, they consider how content takes shape across our experience. They support our products and services by creating content guidelines, writing clear and engaging copy, and collaborating with teams across Enterprise Growth. Our Content Strategists have experience in writing for the web and are strong design thinkers.

DM

Stewards of the Design Process

Design Managers are the liaison between design and the broader business and are responsible for ensuring a high-quality experience for our end users by translating needs into product solutions. They facilitate the conversation between business goals and user needs, manage communication with our partner teams, and ensure timely delivery of design work.
