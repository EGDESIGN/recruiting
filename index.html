Make Change

We're the Enterprise Growth design team at American Express. We create products to meet the needs of people under served by traditional financial services. We're a design team that measures our impact in products shipped and lives changed—not PowerPoint.

What do we make?

We make alternative banking products and services like Bluebird and Serve. We're also helping develop innovative services for emerging markets that'll change the way people in these areas spend and manage their money.

Our work is:

Collaborative

We're a tight-knit group of people whose different areas of focus bring many perspectives to our work. We whiteboard, sketch, mockup, diagram, write, and code to work collaboratively with product owners, tech teams, and business partners.

User Centered

Our users are real people with real needs—our job is to listen to them. We watch how people use our products, then design ways to help them spend and manage money on their own terms.

Process Driven

We have ownership over our work and the flexibility to iterate frequently through Agile. We use sketches and in-browser design prototypes to communicate our ideas—then work as a team to refine and bring them to life.

Our Team

Whether showing off our culinary skills at monthly potlucks or leading mini improv comedy workshops, we're a fun group of folks who are always finding opportunities to weave our passions into our work.

Who are we?

We're designers, strategists, technologists and facilitators who are all strong design thinkers and excellent ambassadors for design across American Express.

There are four different roles on the EG Design team:

XD

Experience Designers

Experience Designers are systems thinkers who lead the design process from start to finish. They guide discovery research and gather insights, develop both functional and visual designers, and communicate those designs to the people who bring them to life. Our XD's are process driven, work with their hands, and come from many different backgrounds.

DM

Design Managers

Design Managers are the liaison between design and the broader business. They bridge gaps between business goals and user needs, manage communication with our partner teams, and ensure we're working efficiently. DM’s shepherd our team through the design process, and make sure our end users have a high-quality experience.

CS

Content Strategists

Our Content Strategists not only shape the tone of our products, they consider where and how our content takes form. To do this, CS’s design content templates, create guidelines, write smart copy, and turn convoluted mumbo jumbo into easy to understand information. Our Content Strategists are strong writers and have a knack for creating consistency and structure.

XT

Experience Technologists

Experience Technologists are front-end developers who bring our designs to life. They co-design the Serve Platform UX in the medium that it'll be experienced, and help us iterate through rapid prototyping. By designing in code, XT’s ensure that all interactions represent their original intent across every device, and that the team is working efficiently.
